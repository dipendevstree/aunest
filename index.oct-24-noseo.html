A Superior Investment:

Jewelbox is an intelligent investment concept that allows you to fetch 4% to 5% higher returns from your conventional balanced mutual fund.

What is balanced Mutual fund?

A balanced fund is the best option for moderate risk takers. Balanced funds, are often called hybrid funds, as they own both stocks and bonds, usually placing about 60% of their assets in stocks and 40% in bonds.

What is Jewelbox ?

Jewelbox concept is all about substituting the 40% of bonds in the balanced fund with gold savings scheme and switching 60% of saving to equity mutual fund. This way, you can build SIP where equity mutual fund strive for higher returns and other hand gold acts as a capital protector on top you can also buy the jewellery by avoiding making charges and wastages. All put together will yield 4% to 5% more.

    Yes, You can participate in multiple jewellers saving scheme with single Jewelbox login.
  • What documents are required for Account Opening?
    Jewelbox account opening process is simple, paperless and instant. You just need to keep below three documents handy: ( either one )
    Aadhaar Card
    PAN Card
    Passport
